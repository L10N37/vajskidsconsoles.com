If you like to switch between games often and really dig these old systems, you may want to consider an ODE. These are devices that replace the optical drive in your console and allow you to load games from an SD card, USB drive, or hard drive.

There are currently 3 polished and commercially released ODEs for the system: the Xstation, the Terraonion MODE, and the PSIO. The MODE is a multi-system ODE, supporting the Saturn and Dreamcast via plug and play, and the PS1 with a similar installation to the Xstation (i.e. not plug and play on these systems).

Though the Terraonion MODE offers more features than the Xstation, anything other than SD card loading can definitely be overkill (i.e. basically the entire library can fit on an SD card).
